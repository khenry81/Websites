Our Role

A Legal Nurse Consultant is a registered nurse with specialized training in legal matters who provides expert consultation on medical and healthcare-related issues within legal contexts.

Popular Services

  • Medical Chart Screening

    We meticulously screen medical charts to assess merit, standards of care, damages, and causation issues — ensuring a thorough and precise evaluation before you commit resources.

  • Expert Witness Coordination

    Locating qualified expert witnesses and attending DME/IME examinations — providing critical support and expertise for your case at every stage.

  • Literature & Standards Integration

    Extensive literature searches with integration of relevant standards and guidelines into your case analysis — enhancing depth, accuracy, and credibility.

  • Medical Event Chronologies

    Detailed, narrative-driven chronologies of medical events identifying potential defendants and critical timelines to strengthen your legal strategy.

  • Comprehensive Written Reports

    Detailed reports serving as invaluable study tools — assisting with interrogatories, deposition questions, and full trial preparation.
